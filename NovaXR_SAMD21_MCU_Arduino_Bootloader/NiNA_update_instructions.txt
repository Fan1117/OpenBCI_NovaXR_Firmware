NovaXR WiFi Module - uBLOX NINA-W102 

Step 1: 
Load the sketch "NINA_W102_Firmware_Checker.ino" on your NovaXR board

Step 2:
Run the board opening the Serial Monitor 
If the firmware is too old (version smaller than 1.2.1)
You need to update the WiFi module firmware
Otherwise you are good to go, stop reading here

Step 3:
Follow the steps here:
https://www.arduino.cc/en/Tutorial/WiFiNINAFirmwareUpdater
